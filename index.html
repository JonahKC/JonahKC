The Untamed Moose

Jonah has a Youtube channel called The Untamed Moose, where he posts an assortment of videos about things that he things are funny and interesting. You can check it out at youtube.com/theuntamedmoose.

JCWYT

JCWYT is a company made by Jonah and a friend. They make a bunch of cool projects. You can view its website at jcwyt.com.

Moose Not Meese

Jonah founded Moose Not Meese, a non-profit with the goal of re-educating "meesers," or people who incorrectly pluralize 'moose.' You can view its website at moosemeese.org.

The Monthly Moose

The Monthly Moose is Jonah's (now discontinued) monthly newsletter covering international moose-related news. You can read the old issues on its website at monthlymoose.moosemeese.org.
